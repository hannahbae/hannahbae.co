Warren College Graphic Design

Role

Skills

Tools

During the 2018-2019 school year, I had the pleasure of being the graphic design intern for Warren College at UCSD.

Warren College committees organize various programs and events for students, particularly those in Warren College. Events range from social mixers to professional events. As the sole graphic designer, I created flyers, stickers, and logos for the Warren College committees.
